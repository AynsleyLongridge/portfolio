Brief

Knowing when, how and where to use images in your work is an essential tool. For this task we were exploring the impact of photography. The brief was to capture micro and macro images taken around University of Ulsterâ€™s Belfast Campus. This was set in relation to a lecture where we were considering the story behind the images, what am I trying to say to the viewer?

Idea

Beginning my research with a mood board I immediately became interested in the idea of reflective images. I took inspiration from artists Irina Ivanova and Timothy McVain where they use reflections to capture an almost double dimension. I wanted to focus on the mirrored glass around the university building and also search for puddles or water near by that could create a reflection for the macro perspective. For the micro images I wanted to capture elements that were unrecognisable.
